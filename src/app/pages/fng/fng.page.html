TIPS FOR YOUR FIRST WORKOUT
  1. When you are first starting, it is important to remember to MODIFY as needed during the beatdowns. If you want to go harder – go harder. If you have not exercised in a long time, slow down. Don’t hurt yourself or even worse, get discouraged. Just show up, work hard and give it your best. That is all that matters. We are not here competing against one another. We are freaking proud of you for showing up at 5:00 in the morning to exercise!
  2. Read our disclaimer, then simply pick a place to post.
  3. Visit our Workouts page to see what site works best for you.
  4. Check out the Lexicon. We have a nick name for everything you do. The Lexicon will give you a definition behind our secret language.
  5. All you need to bring is an open mind and a positive attitude. Wear your regular outdoor workout clothes and, unless you have farmer hands, consider bringing a pair of gloves.
  6. If you are not meeting a friend at your first post, simply show up and introduce yourself as an FNG. You’ll be welcomed.
